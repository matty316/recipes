# protein bread

## ingredients

* 220g King Arthur bread flour 
* 110g vital wheat gluten 
* 15g dough conditioner 
* 300 failife milk 
* 15g white vinegar 
* 7g salt 
* 10g instant yeast 
* 30g honey
* 15g oil

## steps

1. dump all ingredients into the bowl of a stand mixer
2. stir with a sturdy spoon until ingredients are mixed
3. mix with the dough hook until dough passes the **tug test&trade;**
4. remove from stand mixer and cover and rest for 20-30 mins
5. do 2-3 stretch and folds with 20-30 mins in between
6. shape into a loaf and proof in a loaf pan for an hour
7. with 15 mins left preheat oven to 350 degrees 
8. score and brush the outside with eggwash
9. bake for 30 mins or until browned
10. allow to cool for an hour
